## Part 9: Why Prices Vary So Much

### Factors That Increase Cost

**Complexity:**
- Custom features vs standard functionality
- Integrations with other systems
- Data migration from old systems
- Multilingual requirements

**Quality:**
- Custom design vs templates
- Experienced team vs juniors
- Thorough testing vs quick launch
- Documentation and training

**Location:**
- San Francisco agency: Highest costs
- New York/LA agency: Very high costs
- Regional US agency: High costs
- Nearshore (Latin America): Moderate costs
- Offshore (Asia): Lower costs

**Timeline:**
- Rush projects: 25-50% premium
- Flexible timeline: Potential discounts

### Hidden Costs to Watch For

1. **Content creation** - Often not included
2. **Stock photography** - Can add $500-$2,000
3. **Third-party tools** - Email marketing, CRM integrations
4. **Training** - May be extra
5. **Scope changes** - Always cost more
6. **Post-launch fixes** - May not be covered
7. **Hosting setup** - Sometimes billed separately

## Part 10: How to Get the Best Value

### Do's

1. **Get 3-5 quotes** for comparison
2. **Compare apples to apples** - Ensure quotes cover same scope
3. **Ask about what's NOT included**
4. **Check references** before signing
5. **Start with clear requirements**
6. **Consider total cost of ownership** (ongoing + initial)

### Don'ts

1. **Don't choose cheapest option automatically** - Quality matters
2. **Don't pay 100% upfront** - Standard is 30-50% deposit
3. **Don't skip the contract** - Get everything in writing
4. **Don't assume features are included** - Ask explicitly
5. **Don't ignore communication quality** - If they're slow now, imagine during the project

### Best Value Strategies

**For tight budgets:**
- Consider nearshore agencies (same quality, lower cost)
- Start with MVP, expand later
- Use proven platforms (Shopify, WordPress) instead of custom
- Prioritize features ruthlessly

**For mid-range budgets:**
- Balance custom and pre-built components
- Invest in UX design (saves development time)
- Plan for maintenance from the start

**For larger budgets:**
- Invest in proper discovery and planning
- Don't rush - quality takes time
- Budget for ongoing optimization
