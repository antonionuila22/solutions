## What is Nearshore Software Development?

**Nearshore software development** is the practice of outsourcing software development to companies in nearby countries‚Äîtypically within the same or adjacent timezones. For US businesses, this means partnering with development teams in Latin America, particularly countries like Honduras, Mexico, Costa Rica, and Colombia.
